The Phoenix PD355

The Phoenix PD 355 is a 3 Manual Moving Drawknob organ with moving Tab Inter-manual Couplers, and 32 note AGO pedalboard.  The console can be finished to your desire, shown here in optional dark walnut. There are 55 speaking stops, including a wonderful bronze chime. All the mixtures on this and every Phoenix organ are composed of individually sampled ranks, and are voiceable note-by-note and by individual rank just as in their pipe organ counterpart. Note should be taken that this is one more example of the quality of Phoenix organs. All console hardware is high quality and identical with pipe organ hardware ensuring many years of faithful service. The keyboards are of the highest quality, and wooden core or wooden keyboards are available. The specification below is a suggestion for the first of a possible four (4) specifications that can be built into this organ.
