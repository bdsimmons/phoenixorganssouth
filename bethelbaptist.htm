Bethel Baptist Church
Robertsdale, AL

Bethel Baptist Church of Robertsdale, AL chose a Britson D105 LRT to replace their old organ.  The new Britson is equipped with a compliment of doubled amplifiers and speakers totaling 8 channels.  This feature will allow the Britson D105 LRT to move with them into a larger building in the future. 

The organ has been scaled with the voicing software to fit the present need.
