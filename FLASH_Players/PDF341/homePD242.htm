For those interested in a Phoenix Organ for Residential use...
please enjoy the following tracks.

These recordings were done just before the Phoenix PT341 was shipped, and are all very informal recordings.
No attempt has been made at filtering or removing extraneous noises, such as the rattling air vent
you may hear with some registrations.  Presented here with "warts-n-all"!

Recorded in a room with dry acoustics about 16' x 20' in size, a typical room size for most home organs.
Phoenix Organs for home use can be ordered with a very effective Surround-Sound System
which provides avery realistic virtual acoustic for the organ.
