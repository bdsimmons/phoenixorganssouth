Samples of the
Phoenix Organ PT246

These are a few pieces recorded from the Phoenix PT246 with a small Zoom H2 recorder.
Most are recorded on Spec 1, the English/American stoplist.

1) Be Thou But Near - Bach - Phoenix PT246 (Spec 1)
2) Elegy - M. Archer - Phoenix PT246 (Spec 1)
3) Panis Angelicus - César Franck - Phoenix PT246 (Spec 1)
4) Pie Jesu - G. Faure - Phoenix PT246 (Spec 3 - French w/ Velotti Temperment)
5) Sheep May Safely Graze - Bach - Phoenix PT246 (Spec 1)
6) Hymn Prelude - Tune St. Margaret - Phoenix PT246 (Spec 1)
7) Whimsy - D. Wood - Phoenix PT246 - (Spec 1)
