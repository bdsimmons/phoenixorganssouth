Piedmont Cumberland Presbyterian Church
Piedmont, Alabama

Piedmont Cumberland Presbyterian Church made the decision to move up to a new Britson D105a LRT digital Organ.  From their previous 60's vintage Wurlitzer Spinet, in one move, they have made a quantum leap into state-of-the-art digitally produced pipe sound!

After carefully researching the possibility of replacing their  old organ with a newer used organ, they came to the conclusion that considering the relatively small difference in the prices they got for used organs and the reasonable price for the Britson D105, that it was in the churches best interest to buy a new organ.  A huge added benefit is the 10-year factory warranty they now have on their new Britson versus the "as is - no warranty" that they would have had on a used organ.

The organist, Mr. Shannon Ray, enjoyed getting the organ installed just two days before a wedding with just enough time to become familiar with the organ and to get a little practice time in before the event.  We did provide him with some instruction in the features of the organ, an owners manual, and an open invitation to contact us with any further questions.  The church will be served well for many years by this great organ no matter what musical demands are placed on it.... Classical.... Gospel...Traditional hymns... the capability is in the organ
