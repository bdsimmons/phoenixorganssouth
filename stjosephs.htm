St. Joseph's Monastery
Natchez, MS

Nestled neatly into a quiet area of Natchez, under tall pines, is St Joseph's Monastery.  This serene setting is the new home of the Britson D105 LRT.  It is hoped that this organ will be a valued addition to the services at the monastery and a source of inspiration to all who may visit or reside at this place.

 

 

 

Visitors to the Monastery are always welcome to join in worship and monthly programs. Guests will benefit from the quiet, restful atmosphere the monastic community maintains. The experience of the  rhythm of prayer is available to all who wish to join us in praises, whether at dawn for Vigils or at sunset for the chanting of Vespers.

 

 

"...Just a quick note to tell you how very much we are enjoying our new Britson organ.  As you know, we chant most of our Offices throughout the day and this new organ has simply added so much.  Thank you again for all you did to help us get this wonderful instrument in place..."

"May God bless and keep you-"
         Br. Vincent Ignatius, OSB
