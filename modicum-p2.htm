-MODICUM-

Beginnings

 

I didn't take progressive photos of every step in
Modicum's coming of being.
However,
I had enough presence
of mind to take enough pictures
to illustrate her construction
well enough that anyone
can fill in the
blanks
themselves.

 

 

 

 

 

 

...stitches, clamps, and ribs...West System bond......more clamps.... and clamps

...the shape and appearance of a boat in less than 6 hours...

the following day, when the epoxy cured

the boat
was already

incredibly

rigid.

 

 

 

 

...Spruce gunwale,
Virola foredecks and sides,
White Oak seats...
the bottom with keel stem to stern,
covered with six-ounce glass,
all else received West System thrice....

 

 

 


 Waiting patiently for paint and varnish-
