Livingston United Methodist Church
Livingston, Alabana
       Livingston UMC has become a member of the ever growing Phoenix Organ Family! After a diligent search of several digital organ makers, and after living with some loaned organs from other makers, the church ultimately chose Phoenix Organs and D.L. Simmons & Company for there next organ.

This will replace an aging and inadequate old Hammond that was not appropriate for supporting the choir or congregation.  With the new Phoenix Organ PT246, they will have more than ample resourses to draw from for any musical demands which may arise.		 Also, provided as part of this project is a new custom digital Carillon system designed by Phoenix Organs. This will include a dedicated amplifier and sound card within the organ console utilizing the quality digital bell samples from Phoenix' sample library and keying from the organ keyboard for 'real-time' playing.  The Carillon will also be able to be controlled with a dedicated laptop computer and software for replay provided by D.L. Simmons & Company.


Congratulations to everyone at Livingston UMC!
