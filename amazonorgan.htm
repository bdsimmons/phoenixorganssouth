Hymns of Praise: Charles Wesley -- Charles Wesley was one of the most prolific hymn writers of all time, having penned more than 6,500 hymns. Some of them are among the most beloved hymns of Christian history; hymns like "Hark! The Herald Angels Sing" appear in the hymnals of every Christian denomination. Congregational hymn singing was a revolutionary idea in England at the time. Anglican churches had marvelous choirs, but the congregations rarely sang, and then only chanted Psalms to three or four simple tunes.........
