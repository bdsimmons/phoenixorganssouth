Phoenix Organ PT238

The PHOENIX PT238 is a suggested specification for a better than average sized two manual organ.  Nothing is lacking for the performance of almost any music in almost any church setting. Traditionally designed, generously proportioned consoles will make the organist comfortable with this organ at first sight.

Features:

  • Oak Console in stain color of customer's choice

  • Wooden roll top

  • 38 speaking stops including Chimes

  • Complete second specification

  • Independently voiceable mixtures

  • Two (2) 61 note manuals

  • Silent lighted traditional tilting stop tabs

  • 32 note AGO pedalboard with maple caps

  • Bench with storage space (adjustable optional)

  • Lexicon digital acoustic reverb system

  • Lighted capture system with 8 memory levels

  • 8 general thumb pistons

  • 6 thumb pistons per division 

  • General Cancel piston

  • Transposer

  • Bass coupler

  • MIDI in-thru-out

  • Great & Swell Expression pedals

  • Crescendo Pedal

  • Pitch Control

  • 6 X 100 watt speaker/amplifier system
