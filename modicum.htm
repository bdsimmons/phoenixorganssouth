-MODICUM-
\Mod"i*cum\, n. L., fr. modicus - moderate, fr. modus.
A little; a small quantity; a measured supply.
``Modicums of  wit.'' --Shakespeare

The Modicum is the beginning of a long-anticipated journey.  The final destination will be a boat large enough to enjoy a week-end on with family and friends, and safe enough to return home safely even with a bit of fowl weather. 
I have lived my entire life on the Gulf Coast of Alabama (yes, we have a coast!) and have
been fascinated with boats from an early age. All types of boats, from the flat punt-like boats used by old-time oystermen, to sleek, sophisticated sailing yachts.
I've been around water and boats but have never owned one of my own.
Now I do.
It is called "Modicum".

Modicum is my own design based on the well-know "internet plans".  I didn't order any plans, nor  did I draw any plans... I built a boat.  I didn't re-invent the wheel here, but just put some of "me" into a simple boat design. I'm not a boat designer, but I have building and designing experience spanning over 35 years.  I have been a contractor designing houses, an artist/sculptor, a screen-printer, a house painter, a carpenter, a furniture designer/builder, antique refinisher/restorer, a Church Organ Dealer, a web-designer, and even a cook... so why not design and build a boat?

She started as a Cajun Pirogue-type canoe, but since I have always liked the graceful sweeping lines of Dories, some of that was in the mix, accounting for the slight lift in the chine.  This probably reduced the over-all load capacity of the little craft, but, as an artist, I see it as an equal trade. So what have we now? A "P-dory" or a "Do-Rougue". Your choice...It's up to you.
