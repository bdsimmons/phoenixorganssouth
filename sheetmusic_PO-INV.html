Institutional Accounts

Academic institutions and municipal libraries in the US may apply for a Sheet Music Plus Institutional Account. This account allows you to pay by invoice, as well as receive monthly statements of your purchases. You may also share the account with several of your fellow staff members.

An institutional account offers:

  • The option to pay by invoice.
  • Monthly purchase summary statements.
  • Account-sharing among several staff members.

How to Create an Institutional Account

To set up an institutional account, please complete the Institutional Account Application Form below and fax it to 510-420-7190. We will email you when the processing is complete (within 5 business days). If you cannot download the PDF file, email us at invoices@sheetmusicplus.com so we can email or fax a form to you.

Institutional Account Application Form

How to Use Your Institutional Account

Once you place your purchases in the cart, proceed to checkout by entering your account email and password in the Express Checkout fields. If you choose to pay by invoice, we will mail you an invoice when we ship your package. Payment is due within 30 days from when we mail the invoice.

How to Place a Purchase Order Before Submitting Your Institutional Account Application Form

If you would like to place an order before submitting your Institutional Account Application Form, select payment method "check or money order" during the checkout process. When you fill out your Institutional Account Application Form, enter the order number you would like charged to your pending account. Your order will be approved once we approve your account.
