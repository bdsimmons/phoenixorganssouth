Phoenix Organ PT335

Three Manual Lighted Tab Console
     35 Stops / 105 Speaking Voices

True to it's purpose, the PHOENIX PT335 inspires! ...Leading the musician to a greater appreciation of the instrument and it's role.

This Phoenix is perfect for a very nice home practice organ of a small church with a big interest in having the best sound and features.  This organ is available with 2-channels and attractive wall mounted speaker cabinets for those situations where there is little or no room for speakers.  Don't be mis-lead... this organ has an amazing sound for just 2 channels. (more channels optional). 

Overview:
Oak console with paneled sides in one of five standard stain colors, solid oak roll top, 35 speaking stops including Chimes, Three Specifications, three 61-note manuals, silent lighted traditional tilting stop tabs, 32-note pedalboard, bench, Lexicon MPX 110 digital reverb system, Auto Pedal, Auto Solo, 4 thumb pistons to Great, 4 thumb pistons to Swell, 4 thumb pistons to the Positiv, 6 General thumb pistons with 16 memories, General Cancel, transposer, balanced expression pedal to Swell, General Crescendo pedal, 2 channels X 200 watt amplifier system.
