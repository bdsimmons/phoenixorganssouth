NEW
Phoenix II TS-188 Keyboard

The New Phoenix II TS-188 Keyboard will be a perfect fit for home, chapel, small church or the church who enjoys a blended worship style. Included in this PC-based organ/keyboard are very nice grand pianos, exceptional orchestral sounds, and a two complete selections of pipe organ stops. The responsive 88-key keyboard makes it a pleasure to play. With an option to "split" the keyboard, there is great flexibility in combining sounds, and with the programmable presets your combinations will be available at the touch of a button. Unlike any other digital piano, the Phoenix II TS-188 has the ability to combine any of the avaialbe sounds to play together. The touch screen controls make this one of the easiest to use keyboards ever made, and the best looking, too! NICE console.
