Phoenix PD242

The Phoenix PD242 is a Two Manual organ with moving drawknobs.  There are 42 independent ranks with independently voiced mixtures. Detailed voicing of each rank is possible note-by-note, to voice each organ to it's specific surroundings.The console is sturdily built of oak and can be finished in any stain color needed. 

Often organ builders do not include "high-end" features and console hardware on their smaller organs. With Phoenix organs, that is simply not the case. The Phoenix PD242 has a high quality console with the type of hardware that is expected on good pipe organs.  The sound produced by this organ is also first rate. Extremely long digital samples, and advanced software and hardware produce what a pipe organ "purist" expect when pulling a stop and pressing a key.... quality sound. To hear a Phoenix click here.
