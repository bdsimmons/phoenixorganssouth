SUGGESTED SPECIFICATIONS
ORGANS   ·   CONSOLES   · SOFTWARE  ·  SAMPLING   ·   SOUND    ·  SPEAKERS    ·   SPECIFICATIONS

 

The "typical" specifications are offered as suggestions, even though Phoenix Organs does not have a fixed organ model line. If you do not have extensive experience organ design, it is strongly suggested to let Phoenix design the stoplist for you, including building a suggested specification as listed.  Speaker and amplifier options add to the flexibility of each of these suggested organs.  Oak consoles are often used, but any wood or finish combination desired can be built. With a color or wood sample, we can match consoles with design and color of your existing woodwork.

In the moving drawknob (designated as PD###) consoles, the identical high quality drawknobs used in pipe organ consoles are used, with lighted drawknobs as an option. The Tab style (designated as PT###) consoles use trouble-free L.E.D. lighted Rocker tabs; moving tabs are also an option.  At added cost, Phoenix can build consoles using any desired pipe organ suppliers hardware, if you should have a preference for one supplier over another.
